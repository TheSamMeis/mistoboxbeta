About Us

Our mission here at MistoBox is simple: we want to make it easy for you to discover the world's best coffee. We search high and low for the most talented roasters, sample their coffee, and choose 4 each month to send you. Our coffee geeks know the difference between the merely-good and the truly-special so that your only job is to subscribe and enjoy. Along the way, we hope to share what we have learned with you, and if you have any questions we'll be here to answer them. We look forward to serving up a rewarding future of coffee discovery, and are profoundly grateful for your support.
