What it all means

Roaster: A craft focused company we have chosen to feature for their pursuit of perfection and dedication to quality.

Coffee Name: This is the mark of a coffee; the name of the farm, coop or sometimes region where it was grown. We want to share with you the hard work of those who produced each delicious coffee.

Origin: The region or country where a coffee was grown; also known as "terroir", the place and microclimate where each coffee is grown will have a large impact on how the coffee will taste.

Process: The method for removing the external skin, fruit and parchment layers of a coffee cherry. The method which a farmer or miller processes each coffee will heavily influence how each coffee will taste.

Variety: This describes the type of Arabica coffee that was used to produce each coffee. There are about 50 varieties commonly grown around the world, while Ethiopia is native to over 3,000 heirloom varieties. .

Elevation: The altitude at which each coffee grows plays a significant role in the development of coffee. Generally speaking, higher elevation coffees tend to be higher quality.

Tasting Notes: These are some of the most prominent flavors that we taste in each coffee, and hope you will too!

MistoBox Store: The go-to place where you can buy full bags of all of your favorite coffee, roased fresh for your order & shipped to your door.

Roast Date: Coffee freshness is pinnacle to great tasting coffee. Brew coffee within two weeks of roasting to make sure every cup is absolutely delicious.
